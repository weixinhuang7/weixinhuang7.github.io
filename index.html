Weixin Huang

My research focuses on the development of high-performance nanomaterials to respond to the current energy crisis and environmental problem. The research goal is to determine the structure-property relationships of nanomaterials, and this goal would be achieved by combining precise synthetic methods, advanced characterization tools, and theoretical simulation.

The concept of “control, understand, and improve” will be established in developing advanced nanomaterials. Being able to produce uniform structures and identify the ones that are responsible for the catalyst performance is a crucial task (control and understand). Once the active sites are known, it is then possible to engineer catalysts with a higher density of those specific active sites (improve).
